Optimum Nitrogen Application For Maximum Production of Tomato

Deep learning can be applied in tomato production by creating models that can predict the optimal quantity of nitrogen to apply to the soil. Nitrogen is an essential nutrient for plant growth and is often applied in the form of fertilizers. However, applying too much nitrogen can lead to reduced fruit quality and yield, while applying too little can result in poor plant growth. By using deep learning techniques, it is possible to create models that can analyze various factors such as soil type, temperature, humidity, and previous nitrogen application data to accurately predict the optimal amount of nitrogen to apply. This can help tomato farmers to improve the efficiency of their nitrogen usage, reduce costs, and increase crop yield and quality.
